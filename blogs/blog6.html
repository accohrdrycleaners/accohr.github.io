FAQs

Where Is the Cleaning Performed?

Most dry cleaners in Delhi are classified as either dry shops with a central dry cleaning plant or package stores with on-site cleaning. A dry shop is only a drop-off location. The garments are transferred to a secluded area where they are cleaned. Inquire as to the number of dry stores that feed the central plant. Is the central facility exclusive to that chain of dry cleaners, or does it serve other businesses in the area? Large factories strive to keep costs under control, boost efficiency, and turnaround operations as rapidly as feasible. Your garments may not receive specialized care, and you may not receive the best value for your money.

The package retailers maintain their equipment and perform their cleaning. These cleaners are often smaller enterprises with staff that can answer questions about fabric care and listen to your concerns regarding clothing.

Which Cleaner uses dry Cleaning Solvent?

There are several cleaning chemicals and processes available, some of which are more ecologically friendly than others. Additionally, some dry cleaners cycle their cleaning fluids, allowing the re-deposition of dirt from earlier loads. Inquire whether your Cleaner uses freshly purified or freshly distilled fluids on each run.

What Kind of Pressing Equipment Is Used?

Garment pressing can be done manually or mechanically. Machine pressing is frequently used to erase major creases from garments by merely blowing hot steam through them. Steam may be abrasive and cause clothing to lose its form. Machine pressing does not provide the requisite sharp edges and seams. Hand pressing is recommended for delicate fabrics, particularly those embellished with beads or sequins.

How Is Liability for Defective Garments Handled?

Each dry Cleaner makes errors. Things occur. However, it would help to inquire in advance about who will be liable for damaged things and how claims will be handled. Inquire about what happens if a garment is misplaced. A good cleaner should reimburse you for the item's worth.

How Much Does It Cost to Clean a Garment on Average?

Inquire about the cost of a dress, suit, pants, and sweater. Is there a difference in price between a man's suit and a woman's suit? Is wet cleaning billed on a per-pound basis or per-item basis?

Are the pickup and delivery available?

Time is precious, and spending a little extra for pickup and delivery may be worth it to simplify your life. Please inquire about the service area and whether it is convenient for your work and home schedules.
