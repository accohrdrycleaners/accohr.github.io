Same day Drycleaning ?

Adarsh (2020 7 February)

Accohr Drycleaners offers its customers the option to get same day dry cleaning done. Same day dry cleaning is very popular with our clients, as most clients like to receive all Dry Cleaning back as quickly as possible. Additionally, with no extra charge same day dry cleaning is the perfect opportunity for our clients to receive this express dry cleaning service. Accohr Drycleaners collect all dry cleaning in black bags with straps at the top to make sure nothing can come out of the bag.

Our client’s clothes safety is our main priority. That is why all dry cleaning that’s collected has printed invoices/whatsap acknowledgement prior to collecting the garments and is attached to each bag so there’s no chance of confusion or mix ups. Same day dry cleaning allows customers to receive their clothes in a very short time but with quality of the cleaning still high as per our standards.

Dry cleaning is a type of art; all stains that come into Accohr Drycleaners are checked and make sure that the best results are achieved in the first dry clean. All garments are dry cleaned in fresh solvent to make sure no wear and tear occurs to the fabric. Same day dry cleaning is the same price as getting dry cleaning for any other day, as Accohr Drycleaners believe in caring for their customers, and not exploiting them for express charges.

We are able to do this as our factory in Faridabad allows us to operate from central Faridabad which means our drivers don’t have to spend too long on the road and can provide us the garments with ample amount of time to provide this service. Accohr Drycleaners can offer dry cleaning in as little as 6 hours from the point of collection. This makes next day dry cleaners near me the quickest Dry cleaner in central Faridabad.

All dry cleaning at Accohr Drycleaners is steam pressed; steam pressing is one of the most hygienic pressing systems as it kills all potential bacteria that may be on the garment. It is very important for clients to dry-clean their clothes on a regular basis. At Accohr Dryceaners an average client uses us at least once a week for Same Day Dry Cleaning, as they also understand the importance of keeping clothes clean and in healthy condition.

Dry cleaning that’s done on a regular basis also helps to keep the fabric in good condition, which in turn helps the garment to last longer. Accohr Dry Cleaners use same day dry cleaning as a unique selling point for its customers to avail as we are fully aware that with the contingencies that we have put into place we can make sure it is hassle free. Accohr Dry Cleaners gets all garments ready before time as we understand that things can change for customers and they may need the dry cleaning before.

All our orders go into a unique SAAS system where each piece is tagged and also numbered according to the day, so no same day dry cleaning is missed or left behind. We understand the preciousness of time for our customers and hence, strive to make sure all orders are delivered on or before time.
